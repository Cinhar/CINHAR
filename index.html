Mission and Vision

Our mission is to improve community health through advanced imaging data analysis and collaborative research, connecting global experts to advance clinical studies in India. We aim to be a leading scientific society, integrating cutting-edge imaging into healthcare and fostering multi-centric research to enhance health outcomes worldwide. As part of our commitment, we provide free imaging scans to volunteering patients. Their participation supports global research efforts while improving access to advanced diagnostics, particularly in underserved communities.

Operational Model

CINHAR operates through a collaborative model, partnering with global experts to conduct both multi-centric and single-centric studies. Funding is routed through collaborating universities, which allocate resources to CINHAR for project implementation. The network includes technical and clinical consultants from India, ensuring local expertise in project execution. CINHAR covers all imaging and neurological examination costs through research funds, making services free for the community. Each project is led by a Principal Investigator (PI) abroad, supported by consultants in India, as well as coordinators and data analysts at each center. Project details, goals, and budgets are formalized through Memoranda of Understanding (MoUs) and Clinical Study Agreements.
