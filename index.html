Operational Model

CINHAR operates through a collaborative model, partnering with global experts to conduct both multi-centric and single-centric clinical imaging studies. Funding is routed through collaborating universities, which allocate resources to CINHAR for project implementation. The network includes technical and clinical consultants from India, ensuring local expertise in project execution. CINHAR covers all imaging and clinical examination costs through research funds, making services free for the community. Project details, goals, and budgets are formalized through MoUs and Clinical Study Agreements with the partnering clinical centers.
